About the founder

Annet Nakkazi

Annet grew up in a small village in Kyotera district, Uganda, but she got a chance to pursue her bachelor’s degree at the University of Tsukuba (Japan). From there, she joined the University of Oxford for her master’s degree. After, she then joined Uganda Christian University (Uganda) where she lectured for one year, before joining the University of Manchester (UK) for her PhD studies. Annet is passionate about promoting quality education, health and cancer awareness, especially in rural areas. As a person who grew up in an area where school fees were a challenge, and rewards were uncommon, she feels it is energising to reward pupils who are talented in various ways from a young age. It is also good for pupils to get familiar with scholarships/awards, while reflecting on how they have achieved them. You can support by subscribing to our youtube channel, AnnetMaya. Follow her activities on Youtube, LinkedIn, Instagram and Twitter.
